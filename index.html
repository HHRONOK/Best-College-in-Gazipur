The college has a unique Educational Institute Identification Number (EIIN), 109033, which is used to identify and keep track of all educational institutions in Bangladesh. With this number, students, parents, and others can easily find and access information about Gazipur Cantonment College, including its academic programs, admission requirements, and facilities.

The college also has a well-equipped library, modern laboratories, and a spacious auditorium, providing students with the necessary resources to achieve their academic goals.

In addition to academic programs, the college also places a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities, such as sports, cultural programs, and community service projects. These activities provide students with opportunities to develop their leadership skills, form new relationships, and make a positive impact in their community.

Overall, Gazipur Cantonment College is a top-notch institution that is committed to providing its students with a well-rounded education and a path to success. With its unique EIIN number of 109033, it is easily accessible to those seeking information about this exceptional college.
